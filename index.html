Selected Work

    • arXiv PhysRevB.102.064422 (2020)
  • Derived a general theory of magnetically induced polarization and showed why it exists even on centrosymmetric bonds.
    • arXiv PhysRevLett.127.187601 (2021)
  • Predicted room temperature multiferroicity in CuO under pressure.
    • PhysRevLett.129.217601 (2022)
  • Prediction of multiferroic kinks and cross-control of magnetoelectric effect in spiral magnets.
